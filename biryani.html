Biryani Recipe

Biryani

Description

Biryani is a mixed rice dish originating among the Muslims of the Indian subcontinent as a variety of Persian pilaf. It is made with Indian spices, rice, and usually some type of meat (chicken, beef, lamb, or fish) or vegetables.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 1 kg chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2 large onions, sliced
  • 4 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 cup chopped mint leaves
  • 1/2 cup chopped coriander leaves
  • 4-5 green chilies, slit
  • Whole spices (bay leaf, cardamom, cloves, cinnamon)
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil or ghee for cooking
  • Water as needed

Steps

  1. Wash and soak the basmati rice for 30 minutes.
  2. In a large pot, heat oil or ghee and fry the sliced onions until golden brown. Remove half for garnishing.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and green chilies to the pot, saut√© for a minute.
  4. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until soft.
  5. Add chicken pieces, yogurt, mint leaves, coriander leaves, and whole spices. Cook until chicken is tender.
  6. Add soaked rice and water (1:2 ratio of rice to water). Season with salt.
  7. Cover and cook on low heat until rice is cooked and water is absorbed.
  8. Garnish with fried onions and serve hot with raita or salad.
