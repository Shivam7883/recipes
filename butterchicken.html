Butter Chicken

Description

Butter chicken, also known as murgh makhani, is a popular Indian dish made with marinated chicken cooked in a rich and creamy tomato sauce. It is often served with naan or rice.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 large onions, finely chopped
  • 4 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Cilantro for garnish
  • Oil for cooking
  • Water as needed

Steps

  1. Marinate the chicken with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, and salt for at least 1 hour.
  2. In a large pan, heat oil and butter. Add chopped onions and saut√© until golden brown.
  3. Add the marinated chicken and cook until browned on all sides.
  4. Add the tomato puree and cook for 10-15 minutes until the oil separates.
  5. Stir in the heavy cream and garam masala. Cook for another 5-10 minutes.
  6. Garnish with cilantro and serve hot with naan or rice.
