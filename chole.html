Chole

Chole, also known as chana masala, is a popular North Indian dish made with chickpeas cooked in a spicy and tangy tomato-based gravy. It is often served with rice or bhature.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups chickpeas (soaked overnight)
  • 2 large onions, finely chopped
  • 4 tomatoes, pureed
  • 2 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2-3 green chilies, slit
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Cilantro for garnish
  • Oil for cooking
  • Water as needed

Steps

  1. Soak the chickpeas overnight and boil them until soft.
  2. In a large pan, heat oil and add cumin seeds. Once they splutter, add chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and green chilies. Cook for a minute.
  4. Add the tomato puree and cook until the oil separates.
  5. Add boiled chickpeas, turmeric powder, red chili powder, garam masala, and salt. Mix well.
  6. Add water to adjust the consistency and simmer for 10-15 minutes.
  7. Garnish with cilantro and serve hot with rice or bhature.
